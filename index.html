About

Art and design for wildlife conservation.

Mr. Xiang began bird watching and participating in wildlife conservation in 2004. For more than ten years, he conducted bird surveys in the eastern coastal areas of China, especially in Shanghai and Jiangsu Province. He has promoted bird and habitat protection to local governments, media, citizens, and students. His paintings support the protection of wildlife and the ecological environment.
